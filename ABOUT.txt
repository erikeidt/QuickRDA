QuickRDA is an information capture and modeling tool.
It captures facts as concepts and relationships, supports inferencing, query, 
and transformation, such as to GraphViz's DOT language.

This is the core logic engine plus some other components written in Java. 
The QuickRDA project lives here:
	https://github.com/erikeidt/QuickRDA.git

This project depends two projects, found here 
	JEB					https://github.com/erikeidt/JEB.git
	QuickRDA-XLFiles	https://github.com/erikeidt/QuickRDA-XLFiles
